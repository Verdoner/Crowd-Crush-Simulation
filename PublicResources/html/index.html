Crowd crush simulator

This crowd crush simulation is the product of a project done by a group of 2-semester software students at Aalborg University. The focus of this project is to make simulations of previous crowd crush events, by researching and analyzing these events and comparing them. Giving the user the ability to customize the given events, it can help to better understand how important certain tools can be in aiding in preventing these deadly crowd crush events.

Hajj

Over the last 30+ years, several incidents of crowd crushes have occurred during the Muslim pilgrimage, Hajj. This simulation focuses on the event of 2006 where 346 pilgrims died and 289 injured.

Love Parade

Love Parade is an electronic music and dance festival in Germany which was last held in 2010. This event had 21 casulties and more than 600 injured, due to inadequate and overcrowded exit and entry ways.

Itaewon

In 2022 a Halloween event turned deadly when officials where unprepared for the amount of people entering the streets of Seoul, resulting in 159 deaths in the tightly packed Itaewon district.
